- For quality reasons “The Tailor of Gloucester” uses images from https://www.tate.org.uk/art/artworks/potter-illustrations-for-the-tailor-of-gloucester-65490. As a few of the original illustrations are not part of this collection they’ve been omitted from this production as mixing and matching multiple image sources leads to a bad experience.

- SVGs were traced using Inkscape with “Brightness cutoff” and a threshold of 0.450 for original PNGs and 0.650 for original JPGs.

- JPGs have been trimmed and colour-corrected, then saved in /images with a `_processed` suffix. The publication images have been prepared from these by resizing to a max dimension of 800px (retaining aspect ratio) and exporting at 50 quality.

- The scans for “Fierce Bad Rabbit” don’t have a direct publication date, but enough detective work was done to be certain that they’re appropriate to use.

- No scans are seemingly available for “The Story of Miss Moppet,” but enough detective work was done to be certain that we can use original images from a more modern publication.

- Typogrify will change the prices in “The Tale of Ginger and Pickles” to fractions, but they should remain as shillings and pence.
